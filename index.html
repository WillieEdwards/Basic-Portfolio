About Me

Willie Edwards is a student in the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp Full Stack Development program. Willie strives to specialize in front end web development and UX design. He has a history in hand drawn and stop motion animation, which he continuosly works on with his free time. He has a strong belief in positive thinking both personally and professionally and hopes to aid others in the belief of the power of the mind.

While Willie isn't coding or animating, he can be found doing a wide variety of activities that involve both mind and motor skills. He enjoys riding and working on motorcycles, playing and maintaining pinball machines, attending various conventions, making music and cooking.
